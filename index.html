Our Story

At Savoria, we believe in the power of exceptional cuisine to create unforgettable moments. Our passionate team of chefs combines innovative techniques with locally-sourced ingredients to craft dishes that excite the palate and nourish the soul.

Founded in 2010, Savoria has grown from a small family-owned bistro to a culinary destination, all while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and supporting local producers.
